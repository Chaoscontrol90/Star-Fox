Cutting-edge combat technology.

Team Star Fox utilizes only the best and highest quality space combat technology to complete their missions. The team's super-high performance combat ships, the Arwings, come equipped with the state of the art G-Diffuser system , giving the ships the ability to instantly accelerate or decelerate and perform various combat maneuvers. The ship also comes with lasers, which are compatible with twin and hyper upgrades, and smart bombs, which detonate at the pilot's command, causing a devastating explosion.

To learn more about the history and capabilities of the Arwing, you can go to Arwingpedia.
